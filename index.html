India's largest health crisis.

Urban Indians broadly know the risks. The failure is not awareness — it is activation. There is nothing between "feeling fine" and "emergency room."

03 — The Product

What's in the box.

Five components — four physical innovations and one human layer — that together make Swasth Mandal structurally difficult to replicate.

01 / 05
3-in-1 Proprietary Test Strip
A single-prick strip reading glucose, HbA1c trend proxy, and cholesterol simultaneously. Not stocked at any pharmacy. CDSCO Class B certification is our primary regulatory moat — the hardest component for a competitor to fast-follow at comparable economics.
Proprietary IP
02 / 05
Guided BP Sleeve
An upper-arm cuff with embedded posture sensor that detects incorrect arm positioning and prompts correction before reading. Eliminates the leading source of inaccurate home BP measurement and the anxiety that follows incorrect readings.
Hardware Innovation
03 / 05
Cortisol Saliva Stress Strip
A morning colour-change strip quantifying cortisol as a proxy for chronic stress load. First product in the Indian mass market to make stress physiologically measurable at home — at approximately ₹12 per strip, sustainable inside a ₹2,499 quarterly box.
Market First
04 / 05
Junior Health Card
A structured growth-tracking card for children monitoring BMI trajectory and flagging early obesity risk. Addresses rising childhood metabolic disease in urban India and expands addressable footprint beyond adult decision-makers in each household.
Untapped Segment
05 / 05
The Swasth Sevika
A trained local health companion who delivers the box, validates first readings in person, and becomes the family's named contact. Modelled on the ASHA worker network. She is the activation mechanism no D2C-only competitor can replicate — and why projected annual churn sits below 20%.
Core Moat
